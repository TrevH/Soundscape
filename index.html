Soundscape

This is a site for the development of Soundscape - a new interface for musical expression (NIME). It develops the concept and the inspirations to take the source of composition for a digital musician. The purpose is to transport the iPad from small intermit interface to a large visual and public representation of their music and provide them with a more engaging performance.
